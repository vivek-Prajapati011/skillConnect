## Discussion Summary

We decided that SkillConnect will be a hybrid platform connecting local skilled professionals with nearby customers.

Instead of focusing only on bookings, the platform will also provide tools that help professionals grow and manage their business.

---

## User Research

Interviewed:
Local Appliance Repair Technician
Location: Faridabad

### Key Findings

Customers come from:

- Word of mouth
- Service agencies

Communication:

- WhatsApp
- Phone calls

Problems discovered:

- Missed customer requests while busy
- Heavy dependence on agencies
- No proper booking management
- No organized customer history

Interesting Observation:

Technicians already use smartphones comfortably.
The problem is not technology.
The problem is workflow.

---

## Decisions Made

- Product Name: SkillConnect
- Launch City: Faridabad
- Initial Category: Home Appliance Repair
- Marketplace Type: Hybrid
- Core Domain Object: Service Request
- Professional Verification: Optional initially, verified later
