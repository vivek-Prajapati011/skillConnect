# Vision

SkillConnect is a digital business platform that helps local skilled professionals connect with nearby customers while providing tools to grow and manage their business.

Rather than being only a booking platform, SkillConnect aims to become a trusted platform where professionals can build their digital presence, earn customer trust, and manage service requests efficiently.

---

# Problem Statement

Many local skilled professionals rely on:

- Word of mouth
- WhatsApp
- Phone calls
- Local service agencies

to get customers.

This creates several challenges:

- Inconsistent customer flow
- No digital reputation
- Missed service requests
- Difficult booking management
- Dependence on third-party agencies

Customers also face challenges:

- Difficulty finding trusted professionals
- Limited information about service quality
- No simple way to compare professionals
- Uncertainty before booking

---

# Mission

We believe every skilled professional deserves the opportunity to build a successful business.

SkillConnect exists to help professionals attract more customers, build trust, and manage their business through simple digital tools.

---

# Product Principles

1. Solve real business problems.

2. Build for professionals, not developers.

3. Simplicity over complexity.

4. Trust is earned, not claimed.

5. Every feature should either:
   - Help professionals earn more.
   - Save professionals time.
   - Build customer trust.
